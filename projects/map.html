An interactive map guidebook to one perfect day in Zürich — dinner, dessert, and sights, pinned and organized by time of day.

Open full project ↗

Context

Travel plans usually live in a dozen browser tabs. I wanted a single view that answers "what do I do, and where, and when?" — so I mapped a full day in Zürich onto an interactive map, with each stop pinned and grouped by time of day.

What it shows

Dinner and dessert spots, a morning walking tour, and practical tips are placed as map pins you can click for details. Jump to a time of day and follow the route, turning a static list of recommendations into something you can actually navigate.
